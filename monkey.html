MONKEY

Monkeys are intelligent, social mammals that belong to the primate order, the same group that includes apes and humans. They are known for their agility, curiosity, and in many cases, their long tails. Monkeys vary widely in size, behavior, and habitat, and they live in tropical and subtropical regions around the world.
